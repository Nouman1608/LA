Cambridge IGCSE Sociology (0495) gives students their first systematic look at how societies work — and why sociologists often disagree about it. Our teaching makes the perspectives clear and the debates genuinely engaging.

From sociological theory and research methods through family, education and crime to media, inequality and global development, the course rewards students who can explain different viewpoints and support them with evidence. With Core and Extended tiers shaping the depth required, we focus on clear definitions, applied examples and the structured explanation the written papers reward.

Sessions are live and small, giving every student room to connect sociological ideas to the society around them.
