Edexcel International GCSE History (4HI1) explores the modern world through two written papers, asking students to explain change, analyse causes and work with historical sources. Our teaching balances a strong grasp of the period with the analytical skills the exam rewards.

The course covers the era of the world wars, the rise of authoritarian regimes, and the Cold War and its global reach, developing students' understanding of cause, consequence and change over time. Because Edexcel rewards structured argument and source analysis, we practise building clear, evidence-led explanations and evaluating the material the papers provide.

Sessions are live and small-group, with detailed feedback on historical reasoning and written structure.
