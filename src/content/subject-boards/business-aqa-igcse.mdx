The specification runs from business activity, objectives and organisation through marketing, operations, HR and finance to the wider external environment and the basics of strategy. Because the papers favour application and analysis, we spend real time turning textbook concepts into reasoned responses to business situations, supported by the figures and case detail examiners provide.
