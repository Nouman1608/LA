Cambridge IGCSE Chemistry (0620) gives students their first real command of how matter behaves — and pairs it with a distinctive assessment, splitting theory by Core and Extended tier and offering either a hands-on Practical Test or the written Alternative to Practical. We prepare students for whichever path their school takes.

The content moves from atomic structure, bonding and the mole concept through acids, bases and redox into the patterns of the Periodic Table and the foundations of organic chemistry. We give particular weight to the experimental skills assessed in Papers 5 and 6 — observation, measurement and drawing valid conclusions — where careful candidates pick up marks that others miss.

Sessions are live and small, matched to each student's tier so the chemistry is always stretching but never out of reach.
