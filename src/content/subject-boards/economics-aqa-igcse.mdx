AQA Economics (8136) — offered here as our IGCSE-level Economics — introduces the subject through two written papers that test both economic understanding and the ability to apply it to real situations. We teach it to make the ideas stick, not just to pass the exam.

From fundamental concepts and the workings of demand and supply through market failure to the big macro questions of growth, inflation, policy and trade, the course builds a complete first picture of the economy. Because the papers favour application and data interpretation, we practise reading economic information and explaining what it means, with the diagrams and definitions that underpin strong answers.

Classes are live and small, with steady feedback that turns understanding into confident responses.
