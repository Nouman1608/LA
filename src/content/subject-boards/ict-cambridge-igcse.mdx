Cambridge IGCSE ICT (0417) is one of the most hands-on subjects on the timetable — students learn how information systems work and then put that knowledge to use, producing real documents, databases, spreadsheets and presentations. Our teaching keeps theory and practice tightly linked.

The course covers data and information, hardware and networks, software and databases, the development lifecycle and the social, ethical and security dimensions of ICT, while developing genuine practical competence with everyday productivity tools. We give particular weight to the applied skills the practical assessment rewards, so students can work accurately and efficiently under exam conditions.

Sessions are live and small, mixing clear teaching of the concepts with guided hands-on practice and close feedback.
