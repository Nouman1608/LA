Cambridge IGCSE Business (0450) opens up how organisations actually work — why they exist, how they market and finance themselves, and how they manage people and operations. For many students it is a first taste of commercial thinking, and we teach it to spark genuine interest, not just exam readiness.

The syllabus moves from business activity and forms of organisation through marketing, operations and HR into finance and the external environment, with Core and Extended tiers shaping the depth required. A multiple-choice paper rewards breadth, while the written papers reward students who can apply ideas to a given business and explain their reasoning — both skills we build steadily.

Sessions are live and small, giving every student space to connect the syllabus to businesses they already know.
