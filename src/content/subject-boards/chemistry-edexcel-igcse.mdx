Edexcel International GCSE Chemistry (4CH1) covers the subject through two written papers, asking students to understand the principles and apply them to unfamiliar situations. Our teaching keeps both halves of that in balance — solid theory and sharp exam application.

The course builds from atomic structure, bonding and stoichiometry through acids, bases, redox and energetics into the reactivity patterns and organic chemistry that round out the specification. Edexcel likes to test ideas in new contexts, so we practise transferring familiar concepts to unseen problems, alongside the calculation and practical-analysis skills the papers reward.

Sessions are live and small-group, with feedback that steadily tightens both understanding and technique.
