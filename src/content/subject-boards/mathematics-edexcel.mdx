Edexcel A Level Mathematics (9MA0) is assessed entirely through three two-hour written papers, so every mark comes down to how well students communicate mathematical reasoning under timed conditions. Our teachers know the Edexcel mark scheme inside out — they teach *how to write* a correct solution, not just how to reach the answer.

Pure Mathematics runs across Papers 1 and 2 and forms the backbone of the course. We build fluency in algebra and functions early, then layer in calculus — differentiation and integration — which underpins the majority of A Level questions. Proof, vectors and numerical methods receive dedicated sessions so no topic is left as a gap.

Paper 3 splits into Statistics and Mechanics. Our Statistics teaching uses real Edexcel large data set material, and our Mechanics sessions focus on modelling questions where clear diagrams and Newton's law application separate the top grades. Every student works through recent past papers in exam conditions before each series.
