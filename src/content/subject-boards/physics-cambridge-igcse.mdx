Cambridge IGCSE Physics (0625) builds students' understanding of the physical world from the ground up, with a distinctive assessment to match — theory papers split by Core and Extended tier, plus a choice between a hands-on Practical Test and the written Alternative to Practical. We prepare students for whichever route their school follows.

The content moves from measurement, motion and forces through waves, electricity and thermal physics to the fields, nuclear and basic quantum ideas that round out the course. We give real weight to the experimental and data-handling skills assessed in Papers 5 and 6 — taking readings, plotting and drawing sound conclusions — where careful candidates gain a clear edge.

Sessions are live and small, matched to each student's tier so the physics stays challenging without becoming discouraging.
