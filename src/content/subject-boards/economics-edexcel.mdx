Edexcel A Level Economics (9EC0) rewards students who can apply theory fluently to unfamiliar contexts and construct well-evaluated arguments. Our teachers focus on exactly that — building each student's ability to analyse data, assess competing views and write structured responses that earn marks on AO3 and AO4.

Themes 1 and 3 cover microeconomics: markets, elasticity, market failure, business behaviour and labour markets. Themes 2 and 4 cover macroeconomics: UK performance, aggregate demand and supply, global trade, development economics and the financial sector. Papers 1 and 2 draw on two themes each; Paper 3 synoptically draws on all four, which is where our multi-theme revision sessions prove most valuable.

Data response questions appear in every paper. We train students to extract relevant figures, apply the correct economic concept and then evaluate — the step most students miss. Our teachers mark each attempt against the Edexcel mark scheme so students understand precisely where marks are gained and lost before the real exam.
