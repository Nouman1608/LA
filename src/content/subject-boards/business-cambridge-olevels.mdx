Cambridge O Level Business (7081) gives students a clear, structured grounding in how enterprises operate, assessed through two papers that reward applied understanding. Our teaching keeps the subject practical and the answers exam-sharp.

**Note on the syllabus change:** 7081 is the revised syllabus, first examined in 2027. It replaces Business Studies 7115, whose final series is November 2026. If your child sits in 2026 they remain on 7115; anyone starting now will sit 7081. We teach both, and we will tell you which applies to your child before you enrol.

From business activity and organisational forms through marketing, operations, human resources and finance, the course builds a complete picture of the firm and its environment. The revised syllabus adds technology and sustainability in production, and folds digital communication into the people topics — current material that the old syllabus did not cover. Application now carries more marks than it used to, so we drill the habit of tying every point to the specific business in front of you rather than reciting theory.

Lessons are live and capped small, with individual feedback that turns understanding into confident, well-organised answers.
