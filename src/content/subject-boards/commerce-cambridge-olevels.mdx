Cambridge O Level Commerce (7100) is offered only at this level, and it is a genuinely practical subject — how goods and services move from producer to consumer, and the trade, transport, banking and communication systems that make it happen. We teach it so the ideas connect to the real economy students see around them.

The syllabus runs from the nature and scope of commerce through home and international trade, transport and insurance, banking and financial services, advertising and the retail chain, to consumer protection and business ethics. Assessed through two structured papers, it rewards clear explanation and applied understanding, both of which we build through everyday commercial examples.

Lessons are live and capped small, with individual feedback that keeps answers accurate and well-organised.
