Cambridge A Level Business (9609) is as much about judgement as knowledge — weighing options, justifying decisions and arguing a case with evidence, across functions from marketing to finance to operations. Our teaching is built to develop that decision-making mindset.

Students work through business activity and organisational structures into the core functions — marketing strategy, operations, human resources and financial analysis — before tackling the strategy and decision-making that define the A2 material. The assessment runs from multiple-choice to extended structured questions, so we train both precise recall and the longer, well-supported argument that higher marks demand, often through real and topical company examples.

Classes are live and small, with feedback aimed at sharpening analysis and evaluation — the skills that separate a solid script from a top one.
