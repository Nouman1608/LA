AQA Physics (8463) — offered here as our IGCSE-level Physics course — is assessed through two linear written papers spanning the whole subject, from forces and energy to electricity, waves and the atom. We teach it as a connected set of ideas, not a list to memorise.

Students build from measurement, motion and forces towards energy, waves, electricity and magnetism, then on to thermal and nuclear physics. Because both papers can draw on any topic and reward application, we keep revisiting core ideas in fresh contexts and fold in the practical and maths skills the specification expects.

Classes are live and small, with close feedback that catches misconceptions early and turns them into secure understanding.
