Edexcel International GCSE Physics (4PH1) covers the subject through two written papers, asking students to understand the principles and apply them to unfamiliar problems. Our teaching balances conceptual clarity with the calculation and exam technique the papers demand.

The course develops from forces, motion and energy through waves, electricity and magnetism into thermal and nuclear physics, with a strong quantitative thread throughout. Edexcel rewards candidates who can apply equations and ideas to new situations, so we practise exactly that, alongside the practical-analysis and data skills woven through the specification.

Sessions are live and small-group, with feedback that steadily sharpens both understanding and problem-solving.
