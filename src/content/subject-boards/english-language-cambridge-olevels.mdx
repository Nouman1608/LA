Cambridge O Level English Language (1123) is, above all, a test of clear, accurate and effective written English — assessed through two papers that reward strong composition and sharp reading comprehension. Our teaching is built to make every student a more confident, controlled writer.

We work on the core skills the exam demands: structuring and developing a piece of writing for a given purpose, writing with accuracy and range in grammar and vocabulary, and reading unseen passages closely enough to answer comprehension and summary questions precisely. Regular guided practice in both writing and comprehension steadily lifts the quality and reliability of students' work.

Lessons are live and capped small, with detailed, individual feedback on each student's writing — the fastest way to turn good ideas into well-expressed, high-scoring answers.
