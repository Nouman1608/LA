Cambridge IGCSE Biology (0610) introduces students to the living world with real rigour — and a distinctive assessment to match, combining theory papers split by Core and Extended tier with a choice between a hands-on Practical Test and the written Alternative to Practical. We prepare students for whichever route their school follows.

We pay particular attention to the practical and data-handling skills examined in Papers 5 and 6, where careful observation and method earn marks many candidates leave on the table.

Sessions are live and small, pitched to each student's tier so the work is always challenging but never overwhelming.
