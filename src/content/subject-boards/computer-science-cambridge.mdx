The assessment spans multiple-choice and structured questions across AS and A2, with problem-solving and pseudocode at its heart, so we practise designing, tracing and refining algorithms until that thinking becomes automatic.

Classes are live and small, with hands-on problem-solving and individual feedback on every student's code and logic.
