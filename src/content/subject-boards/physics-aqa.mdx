With AQA A Level Physics (7408), everything comes down to three written papers at the end — no modular resits, and the practical skills built up over two years feed straight into the exams. Connected understanding and physical intuition are what carry students through, and our teaching is built to develop them.

Rather than isolating topics, we link them: how the mechanics of forces and energy underpins later work, how fields and electromagnetism connect, how the same problem-solving moves recur from waves to nuclear physics. The required practicals are treated as genuine investigations, since their techniques and the reasoning behind them surface across all three papers.

Lessons are live, capped small, and heavy on problem-solving and past-paper application, building the fluency and stamina a linear assessment demands.
