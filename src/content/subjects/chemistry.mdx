Private school-teacher tuition is commonly quoted at ₨ 30,000–60,000 per subject on Pakistani tutoring platforms, so small-group classes are the most cost-effective route to an experienced teacher. Yes. Classes are board-specific — Cambridge O Level 5070, Cambridge IGCSE 0620, Edexcel 4CH1, AQA 8462, and A Level 9701, YCH11 and 7405 — taught from the official specification documents.

**Group classes or 1-to-1 — which is better for chemistry?**
Small group classes suit most students and cost less. 1-to-1 works best for students targeting an A* on a tight timeline or rebuilding weak foundations. The free demo class helps us recommend the right format.
